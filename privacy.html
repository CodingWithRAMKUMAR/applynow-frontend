Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

ApplyMore respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. The job links you submit are stored only in your browser's local storage and are not sent to our servers. No cookies or tracking scripts are used except for Google AdSense, which may place cookies for personalized ads.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Data Security

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All job listings are fetched from public APIs and are not associated with any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.
